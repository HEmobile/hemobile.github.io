  • Wireframe sketching
    Client and team design the pages as they imagine that the project will be developed. See more!

    Wireframe sketching

    Monday

    The objective of this stage is to extract everyone's main ideas (client's and team's) and, through communication agree on what needs to be done.

    When we speak of wireframe sketching, we literally mean a drawing of what you imagine will be like your interface. Simple, with pen and paper. Check out an example!

    1. All team and client sketch, individually, the interfaces as imagined;

    2. The sketches reflects the application's flow. How should be the interaction between users and the application, which elements are mandatory and so on;

    3. Everyone presents their sketches and there's a discussion regarding the best wireframes;

    4. Creation of a final sketch, which becomes the input for the next step.

  • Breaking tasks
    The team breaks the final sketch in design and programming tasks, technically discussing how to proceed. See more!

    Breaking tasks

    Monday

    The main objective here is technically detail how to transform the idea into concrete features.

    1. After the team discusses the wireframes, is created a step-by-step walkthrough for each wireframe. It is called "stories", where inside of each one, there are lots of tasks;

    2. Our team discusses the stories, the relationship between them and the product objective, finalising with the breaking of tasks for each story;

  • Prioritizing the tasks
    Client prioritizes the tasks to be executed along the week. See more!

    Prioritizing the tasks

    Monday

    After you have mastered all tasks and the relationship between them, it is time to prioritize and define which are the most important to be developed during the week.

    1. The client puts in priority order all tasks;

    2. The team helps the client to prioritize, pointing out complexities of each task, aiming to simplify the scope;

    4. At the end of priorization, both the client and the team know what will be developed during week;
